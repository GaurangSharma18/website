Projects

AGCO Power deisel engine assembly
Collaboration between the feild of Industrial automation and AI is booming at an exponential rate. Robots are aiding industry by reducing human time efforts, plus increasing precision in traditional tasks. However, It always struggled with a few limitations like lightning, noise, dirt, and more which limits the performance in various scenerios. In this task I performed object and target detection on ACGO Power diesel engine using Franka Panda robot and Intel realsense camera. This task contributed to a bigger project on engine assembly in the Tampere University, Robolab.
Below are the images of training dataset and its augumentation using Albumentation library.

Facebook's detectron2 and Yolov5 were utilized on the project, Below are the results of object and target detections and their respective masks using detectron2. Mask RCNN helped in predicting accurate masks and the recieved confidence score was above 95% for major of the instances.
Dental disease detection
AI has the ability to significantly enhance patient care and transform the medical and dental industry. It is being researched for a number of reasons in dentistry, including the identification of normal and pathological structures, diagnosis of disorders, and the prognosis of treatment outcomes. In WP1, health and wellbeing, AI hub collaborated with Futudent to pilot and demonstrate the potential of AI tools in the field of dentistry. We sought to predict dental diseases.
